Hi folks,
 
I love the work featured on proce55ing
 
I run a record label called Audiobulb Records.... www.audiobulb.com
 
We release 'exploratory electronic music' as well as hosting art images on our site. I have plenty of hosting space and am looking for flash or java artists who wish to have their work exhibited on our site. We have room for small pieces on the front page - right hand menu @ a size of 150 pixels by 150 px.
 
Alternatively - if you have larger works I could set up a whole page for this & invite artists to provide the abcking music for the visuals.
 
Another area within the site where further visuals could be incorporated is the 'sine-wave decomposition' section where further works featuring the evolving image of the sine wave could be incorporated.
 
Anyway - an invite to all the artists who have work and wish to have it exhibited in other areas. Get in touch for more info or to show me possibilities....
 
All the best David Newman
contact@audiobulb.com

Newsletter pasted below - - - -
 
Audiobulb is ready for 2004 with a range of planned exploratory audio releases, visual presentations and virtual studio creations. During this year you will have the chance to purchase our first commercial CD releases, including compilation projects and individual artist albums.
 
We start the year with a strong audio and visual offering - bringing together the dynamic images of zevan rosser with the audio of marion. This interactive piece invites the listener to manipulate shapes in real time to the accompaniment of marion's track 'quilt'. zevan rosser has used the coding possibilities of 'proce55ing' to create this dynamic piece. More of zevan's work can be found at www.shapevent.com with further information about proce55ing available at www.proce55ing.net
 
We are also proud to display the extraordinary work of Evgeny Kiselev an artist from St.Petersburg, Russia. Kiselev brings together organic and psychedelic freeform imagery. Further works can be found at http://ekiselev.spb.ru
