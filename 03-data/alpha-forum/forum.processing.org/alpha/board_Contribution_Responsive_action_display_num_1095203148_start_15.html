Markavian, you can have the honour of naming the thing ... sparky it is
 
The high score and menu stuff you mentioned are all things that I would like to fix, but I absolutely despise doing GUI work Well that and the fact that I've been competely snowed under at work mean I haven't touched it for ages. My intention is to drag a lot of the high score stuff on the site into the applet. The idea being that you can see the current and all time top scores and see where you placed ... similar to the way the highscores are presented in some of the miniclip games.
 
You can turn on anti-aliasing in the game by pressing the s key. I'm not overly fond of it though - I prefer the jaggies and it tends to slow it down a little too much for my liking.
