Heal the World
 
With the looming news of war just around the corner, the world is
  at peace in prayer.
Let us do our part in healing this war-torn world and help
  in attaining world peace.
Each must start at his/her own backyard.
  At his/her own home.
 
http://studio.instituteofmedia.com/experiments/heal/
 
-----
Notes:
- Demonstration of simultaneous signals for visual and auditory,
  specifically the concept of 'play' and 'pause'
- Demo of MIDI playing in Java. MP3, among others are bandwidth
  hogs; not suitable for low-bandwidth communities
- Progress bar to indicate percentage of song covered
- Would be nice if someone can synch the MIDI playing with visual
  elements. I can't seem to find a good method that returns
  something useful for this
