setup: p-068, w2k, qt6.5.1, winVdig combo
example: hernando barragan's 'pixels'
 
when putting my p0068-folder on the win.desktop, that is:  
C:\Documents and Settings\Administrator\Desktop\processing-0068
p5 freezes and the javaw.exe process has to be killed.  
 
putting the p0068-folder to something 'simple' like:
d:\processing-0068
everything is fine.
 
needless to say: the folders are copies (no changes) and i usually start up processing.exe and not run.bat.
 
this might also be a cause for some of the quicktime-problems that have been posted in the group. i strongly suggest that this is written down somewhere prominently (install.txt/readme.txt?) if it can be replicated by someone else.
 
if it's just me who's dumb, blind & stupid enough to even try running p5 fron such akward locations, please disregard this post.
