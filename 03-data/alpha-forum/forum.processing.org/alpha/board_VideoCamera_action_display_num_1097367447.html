I have used an old logitech webcam, iSight, and a several different firewire DV cameras. The DV cameras are far and away the best in terms of image size and quality.  
The webcam is fine if the lighting is good and you are not doing motion detection. When I tried doing motion detection with the webcam the amount of noise in the video created a lot of false positives.  
 
The iSight was unusable. The image quality was good but it gave me 1fps in P5 v.68  Maybe that's changed with updates to the iSight firmware and/or java updates, but I haven't checked.
