Re: More files open

yeah, the tabbed editor is a toss-up. we're trying to figure out what's best in terms of how much time we spend building a better editor vs. just what's needed for the majority of users.  
 
our general feeling is that for people who are picky, they'll use their editor of choice anyway, so we're betting off spending time on other parts.
 
but it's a delicate balance between making sure that the editor is 'usable' and useful, versus underpowered to the point of frustration. for instance, i think find/replace is prolly a necessity. tabs i'd love to have (i like tabs a lot personally) but i'm not sure if it's needed for the built-in editor (it starts breaking the single sketch model, so there are repercussions). somewhere in-between was another query for a contextual menu cut/copy/paste.. which side does that fall on?
